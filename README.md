# plant_care_hub
This project is an AI-powered Plant Care Hub designed to help users monitor and maintain plant health through intelligent diagnostics. By simply uploading a photo, the system leverages AI features to analyze the plant's condition and identify potential issues or diseases. 

## 🌱 Plant Care Hub

**Plant Care Hub** is an AI-powered web application designed to help enthusiasts monitor, maintain, and rescue their indoor and outdoor plants. By leveraging machine learning, the platform allows users to upload photos of their plants to receive an instant health diagnosis and actionable care advice.

---

### 🚀 Features

* **AI Disease Diagnosis**: Upload a photo of a leaf or plant to identify pests, diseases, or nutrient deficiencies using integrated AI features.
* **Plant Dashboard**: A centralized hub to track growth stages and manage your digital garden.
* **Care Reminders**: Personalized notifications to ensure your plants are watered and fertilized on schedule.
* **Responsive Design**: A clean, modern interface built with HTML5 and CSS3 that works seamlessly across desktop and mobile devices.

---

### 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript.
* **AI/ML**: Integrated AI models for plant health classification.
* **Deployment**: Compatible with platforms like Streamlit for machine learning model hosting.

---

### 📝 How to Use

1.  **Get Started**: Navigate to the `index.html` and click **"Get Started"** to access your dashboard.
2.  **Diagnose**: Select **"Try Diagnosis"** and upload a clear image of your plant.
3.  **Review**: Receive an AI-generated report on the condition of the plant and recommended next steps.
